# RPG & MMORPG Features List

## Core RPG Features
- Character creation & customization
- Stats & attributes (STR, DEX, VIT, etc.)
- Leveling & experience
- Skills & abilities
- Inventory & equipment
- Item drops & loot
- Quests & quest log
- Dialogue & choices
- Combat system (turn-based/real-time)
- Map & exploration
- Save/load system
- Achievements

## MMORPG Features
- Multiplayer (online)
- Chat system
- Guilds/clans
- Parties/groups
- Trading & auction house
- PvP (player vs player)
- World events
- Persistent world
- In-game economy & currency
- Social features (friends, mail)
- Anti-cheat & moderation
- Server-side save/progress
